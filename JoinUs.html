General Member

Step 1: Register on this website to become a site member;

Step 2: Fill out the Google Form Registration below

Step 3: You will receive welcome letter/instructions from your chapter leader

Benefits

  • Unlimited FREE access to all resources, programs, blogs, and events at College Leap and your chapter;
  • Regular newsletters from your chapter and College Leap;
  • Eligible to apply for a volunteer award for your community service hours.

Project Associate

Project Associate

An Internship position in one of the EIGHT project councils under project department to help build national programs and events (i.e. NBPC, TSPS, etc.):

  1. Business Council
  2. Social Sciences Council
  3. Art & Humanities Council
  4. STEM Council
  5. Transfer Council
  6. Volunteer Council
  7. Internship Council
  8. General Resources Council

Benefits

  • Work side by side with College Leap leadership team to launch national programs and campaigns;
  • Certificate & Potential Recommendation letter;
  • Grow with College Leap as a national organization.

Requirements

  • A minimum of 10 hours per week (depends on the project you work for);
  • Entrepreneurial & willingness to learn.

Chapter Leader

A non-paid internship at College Leap.

Six leadership positions.

Interview required.

Position description HERE

Benefits

  • Intern offer, onboarding & training, CPT & OPT-sponsored;
  • Certificate, exclusive resources, coffee chat;
  • Networking, hands-on experience.

Requirements

  • 10-hour weekly commitment;
  • Weekly meeting & sync-up meeting.

You can apply even if your college doesn't currently have a chapter or all chapter positions are filled in your chapter.
