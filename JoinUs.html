Project Associate

An Internship position in one of the EIGHT project councils under project department to help build national programs and events (i.e. NBPC, TSPS, etc.):

  1. Business Council
  2. Social Sciences Council
  3. Art & Humanities Council
  4. STEM Council
  5. Transfer Council
  6. Volunteer Council
  7. Internship Council
  8. General Resources Council

Benefits

  • Work side by side with College Leap leadership team to launch national programs and campaigns;
  • Certificate & Potential Recommendation letter;
  • Grow with College Leap as a national organization.

Requirements

Gamma Mu Psi Society

Gamma Mu Psi Honor Society

Gamma Mu Psi (GMP) is a National Honor Society started by College Leap (NPO) for international transfer students at 4-year universities in the U.S to gain career development resources, networking opportunities and give back to their community.



If you want to start a GMP chapter at your university, please contact us for more information.
