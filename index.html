Community College Chapters

Student organizations at community colleges to support local student bodies

College Leap establishes chapters at community colleges to fulfill its mission on the campus level. All College Leap chapters are positioned as "Premium Transfer Clubs" on campus to provide transfer-related resources and information.

Each College Leap chapter is managed by an executive board and an advisor to organize chapter projects, recruit members, and promote College Leap programs.
