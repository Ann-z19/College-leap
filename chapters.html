COLLEGE LEAP CHAPTER

WHAT IS LEAP CHAPTER

College Leap Chapters are student clubs at community colleges to provide transfer-related resources and information to students on the campus level. All College Leap chapters should be considered as
"Premium Transfer Clubs" on campus

Here are four main components of a College Leap chapter:

Chapter Leaders/Committee

3–10 student leaders to manage and operate a chapter;
Committees are set up under the chapter leaders

Chapter Advisors

One full-time faculty member or staff to serve as advisor

Chapter Projects

Programs and events organized by chapter leaders and are open & free to all students on campus

College Leap Projects

Chapters will promote College Leap projects to their members and communities

BENEFITS

Having a College Leap chapter means:

Administrators

  • A pool of online student activity resources and information
  • Higher transfer and student success rate

Enrolled Students

  • Access to resources to improve transfer profile
  • An online community to receive support and information

Club Leaders

  • Leadership positions with mentorship and guidance
  • An exclusive community for all chapter leaders
