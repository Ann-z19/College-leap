FIND YOUR FIRST STARTUP INTERNSHIP

IAAS (Intern-At-A-Startup) is a program for community college students to find and apply for internship and cofounder positions at startups and student projects respectively.

Student Projects

Students who work on their own projects could search for founding team members or important roles via IAAS

Startups

Startups that are officialy registered and more established will hire interns via IAAS.

❮ ❯

THE IAAS

We understand it would be quite challenging for community college students to find internships at startups or join some students projects at the very early stage So we've built IAAS to help with these processes
